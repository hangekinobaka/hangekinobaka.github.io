『Indie』ShoomShoom

Shoom Shoom

An independent game demo created by me and my friend オウシン(Chen Wang)

The game itself is still under development, we will strive to make a complete gaming experience.

We have created a complete world view and story framework for the game, and carefully designed characters, story, and gameplay. We hope you will enjoy it.

Click   Here   to play!

Or check the Github repo here  

The game is about a small mouse named Shoom Shoom, who lives in the underground city in a steampunk world.
He uses his homemade steam machinery and weapons to smuggle goods for customers in order to survive.

How to Play

You can use wd or ←→ to move left and right.
Use shift to run.
Use space to jump.

Project Structure

I use Unity3D as the game engine.
Also includes these functionalities:

  • URP
    To make the scene more beautiful.
    Add ShaderGraph to create special render effect(like the water and the shadow of water reflected on the wall behind)

  • UniRx
    Reactive state control.
    Script sequence control.

  • DoTween
    tween animation.
    For example the camera movement.

  • Cinemation
    Camera control.

  • Spine Unity Runtime
    For implement the spine animations I have created.

I also created the spine animations for shoom shoom and his enemies. (Will create more in the future…)
