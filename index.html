Sleepy Scene Tool (for Unity)

Don’t you feel that it is frustrating when you have an enter scene so that every time you need to start the game, you gonna have to go to that enter scene then hit the start button.

Isn’t it annoying that your scene file hidden in the project hierarchy and you have to use the search bar to find the scene you wanna use.

In the begining I thought that I can find a good plugin for this, but no, either are they too hard to use or they are expensive.

So I thought, why not create my own one. So, boom, voila, the Sleepy Scene Tool is here:

Stupitoma

A stupid tomato is trying to across the road without dying.

Click Here to play!

Or check the Github repo here  

This is a game demo using CocosCreator. I have draw the sprites and programmed the whole stuff. Click to start. Once you clicked, your toma will go forward to across the road. If you click the left side of the screen, your toma will turn left and stop (as it is stupid, not able to walk and see in the same time), meanwhile you shall not see the right side. Vice versa.
